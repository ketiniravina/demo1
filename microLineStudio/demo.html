Microline studios is a indie game development company based in Bengaluru,india. We are a bunch of passionate individuals developing games on creative, fun, and innovative ideas.We extend development services to indie as well as large game studios.

OUR TEAM



Kiran Kodi

Founder || IIT

Kiran is an extremely talented coder, graduated from IIT. Kiran has been designing, optimizing our games, and adding a suite of all-new exciting features for games. He is the lead artist and animator. His ability to think and solve problems has helped in clearing difficult roadblocks and has set our paths into our future endeavors.


Pavan Kodi

Co-Founder

Pavan is our game designer and developer at Microline. He's been designing game levels, creating crazy worlds for people to poke around in and explore. He is a self-taught 3d modeler, animator and also handles the business and pr section of the company. His continuous effort towards the goal had kept us all aligned in our journey.  


Murali Krishna

Co-Founder

Murali is an expert game programmer. He designs our game's networking and AI modules for players to connect with each other and he also designs our gameplay environments. He has past experience as a machine learning engineer, building end-to-end models for clients for almost over 3 years. His passion and love for gaming drove him to co-found this company.
